BermudA TriAngle


Instructions


This is a 2 player game (can be played with more if u choose to).

The goal is to match the shape of your triangle to the given triangle. Each click randomly resets the shape of your triangle, if you get one of your points close enough, it will fix in position. The first player (or team) to get all 3 points on the triangle wins.

Press the spacebar to reset.
